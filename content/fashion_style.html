Fashion and Style

Crimson Accord strives to portray a ‘noir’ feel in game. The populace of New Amsterdam has just recently recovered from a devastating attack that claimed the lives of thousands in the ensuing chaos that occurred after the bombing of the United Nations.

‘Dark elegance’ would be a good short descriptor as ladies wearing netted face veils mingle with sharply dressed men in black suits, as if the entire city were still in mourning over the tragic events that occurred.

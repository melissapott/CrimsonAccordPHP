Home Automation

Over the past decade, there has been a steady increase in automation technology filtering in from the manufacturing sector into home use. These days it's common to see lights, AC & heating, and window shades all controlled by your smartphone, or a computer-controlled refrigerator that automatically prepares dinner on a pre-programmed schedule. City administrators also recognize the need to reduce energy consumption while becoming more efficient in the way they use various systems throughout their facilities, so recently there has been a push by New Amsterdam officials to lure robotics and automation manufacturing firms to the city with offers of hefty tax breaks, as well as a major initiative to install similar devices in public spaces such as airports, convention centers, and concert halls...

Community Spotlight

SAPLING is the nation's largest community organization of low and moderate-income families, working together for social justice and stronger communities. Since its founding in 1997, SAPLING has grown to more than 175,000 member families, and has organized in 850 neighborhood chapters in 75 cities across the U.S. and in cities in Canada, the Dominican Republic and Peru. Their accomplishments include successful campaigns for better housing, schools, neighborhood safety, health care, job conditions, and more. SAPLING's members participate in local meetings and actively work on campagins, elect leadership from the local populace...
